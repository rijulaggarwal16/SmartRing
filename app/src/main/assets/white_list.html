White List Setting

With a white list, you can allow important coontacts reach you, even when you block others. It can only work when it is enabled, as well as the profile for a particular time slab is selected to be 'No Call Silent'.

Enable/Disable Feature:

You can find the general enable/disable feature when you open settings from the menu.

Edit the list:

This screen is accessed when you click on the 'White List' row in the preferences screen.

You can add contacts to white list by clicking on the add contact button. You can remove the contacts as you wish by clicking the remove icon. After editing is finished, click save to store the changes.
